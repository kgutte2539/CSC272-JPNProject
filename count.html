Counting in Japanese

When it comes to learning how to count numbers in Japanese, it may sound difficult but like other languages it does come with patterns which actually isn't that hard. Japanese counting consists of algorithm literally from 1-10 and as long you memorize 1-10 and the digits, it will make counting easy.
